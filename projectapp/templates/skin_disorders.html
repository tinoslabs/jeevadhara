Skin Disorders

▶️ Non Healing Wounds and (Chronic ) Ulsers of the Skin
Definition : Inflammatory changes in a wound, with infection.
Causes : Local infection usually associated with trauma.
Symptoms : Heat, swelling, pain, redness, fever.
▶️ Furunculosis(Boils)
Definition : A suppurative swelling of the skin and subcutaneous tissues,Usually hard and round, which may discharge.
Causes : An infection, usually circulatory due to hyperglycemia(Diabetes mellitus)
Symptoms : Throbbing pain, redness, fever, glandular enlargement, May become a carbuncle.
▶️ Acne(pimples)
Definition : A chronic inflammation of the sebaceous glands of the skin, with The eruption of hard elevations and red areas of infection,(usuallyOf the face).
Causes : Carbohydrate excess diet; constitutional, lack of hygiene, anxiety.
Symptoms : Discomfort, irritation, infection, pain, discharge.
▶️ Warts
Definition : A benign overgrowth of the outer layer of the skin.
Causes : Various. Often emotional or hereditary. Associated with the Sycosis miasm.
Symptoms : Warts often disappear without treatment. Those on the sole of the Foot are especially painful.

Endocrine Disorders - hypogonadism ,Sub Fertility

Definition : A decrease in the production of spermatozoa and secretion of androgens.
Causes : The functional failure of one or both testes.
Symptoms : If occurring before puberty, the external genitalia and secondary sex Characteristics fail to develop. If after puberty, there is a gradual loss Of public hair and the external genitalia will atrophy. Psychological Changes may also occur.

Psychiatric Disorders -Neurasthenia (Fatigue)

Definition : Tiredness, lassitude and absence of energy.
Causes : Many causes; failure to lead a regulated life, anaemia, infection, Post-surgical, endocrinal, convalescence.
Symptoms : Lack of energy and exhaustion.
