Disorders of women- Gynaecological Disorders

▶️ Oligomenorrhoea ( scanty flow at periods )
Definition : The periods are regular, but the flow is scanty, and short-lasting.
Causes : Constitutional, adolescence, anaemia, debility, hormonal or Pre- menopausal.
Symptoms :The menstrual flow is scanty and short lasting.
▶️ Delayed Puberty ( In females )
Definition : when the normal twenty-eight day cycle has not commenced by About the age of fourteen years.
Causes : Constitutional, nutritional, endocrine, anaemia, stress and Nervousness; (imperforate).
▶️ Irregular Menustration
Definition : The periods are unpredictable and there is not the normal twenty- eight Day cycle, but a variation in length of cycle and /or period of the flow.
Causes : Fibroids, pre-menopausal, stress, anxiety, convalescence, traveling(jet- lag), infection, anaemia, malnutrition.
Symptoms : The periods are irregular and unpredictable.
▶️ Leucorrhoea
Definition : A vaginal discharge, usually white or clear.
Causes : Chronic inflammation of the mucosal lining of the vagina or the cervix
Symptoms : A vaginal discharge, with irritation, pain and discomfort.

Obseric disorders

▶️ Morning sickeness ( vomiting of pregnancy , Pre-eclapsia )
Definitions : Nausea and vomiting, usually in the early weeks of pregnancy. (Pre- eclampsia occurs later).
Causes : Psychological factors or hormonal imbalances.
Symptoms : Persistent early morning nausea and vomiting during the first three Months of pregnancy. Lack of appetite.
▶️ Infertility ( Repeated abortions or Miscarriages)
Definitions : The failure to conceive after continual attempts to procreate over a Prolonged period.
Causes : Causes are multiple; the problem may be in the male or the female.Cause may be determined by the sperm profile,
Symptoms : Pregnancy fails to occur. Repeated miscarriages.
▶️ Painless delivery - Relief of pain at Childbirth
Definitions : The three stages of explusion of the infant and placenta constitute Delivery.
Symptoms : Pain, contraction, bearing down pains.
▶️ Delivery miscarriages, Abortion, Pre-mature delivery
Definitions :The spontaneous and premature termination of pregnancy.Usually in the early weeks.
Causes : From acute shock, and emotional disturbances; hormonal imbalance or From infections.There may be an abnormality of the developing foetus, or of the uterus.
Symptoms : Hemorrhage, regular uterine contractions, abdominal pain, collapse. Fear of death.
